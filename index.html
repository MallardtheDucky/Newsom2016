The American Renewal Platform

A comprehensive, investment-driven strategy for a prosperous and secure Commonwealth.

The American Worker First Act & Progressive Taxation

We will restructure our tax code to reward the middle class and ensure large corporations pay their fair share, fueling investments in the American worker.

  • **Corporate Tax Increase:** Raise corporate tax rate from 38% to **42% for large corporations**.
  • **Minimum Wage:** Raise the Federal minimum wage to **$8.50/hour** through a structured phase-in.
  • **Mandatory Paid Time Off:** Establish **4 weeks paid vacation** and **8 weeks paid family leave** for ALL workers.
  • **Federal Jobs Guarantee:** A commitment that **"Every American who wants to work, works"**.
  • **Retraining Vouchers:** Provide **$10,000 retraining vouchers** for displaced workers in transitioning industries.
  • **Apprentice America:** Fund **500,000 paid apprenticeships** to close the skills gap.

The American Health Security Act: Universal Coverage

Healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Our plan creates a resilient, cost-effective system guaranteeing care for everyone.

  • **Single-Payer System:** Phase in a **single-payer system over 4 years**.
  • **Medicaid Expansion:** **Immediate expansion of Medicaid** to cover all unemployed citizens.
  • **Drug Price Negotiation:** Implement **Federal negotiation of prescription drug prices** to lower costs immediately.
  • **Funding:** The $180 Billion annual cost will be funded through a modest **payroll tax increase**.

The Opportunity Investment Initiative: Pre-K to College

Investing in education is the best stimulus. We are raising the federal role to ensure quality education is guaranteed regardless of zip code.

  • **Teacher Program:** Create a Federal jobs program placing **100,000 teachers in underserved areas**.
  • **Pell Grants:** Expand **Pell Grants by 40%** to assist low-income students.
  • **Free Community College:** Make **community college tuition-free** for all residents.
  • **Universal Pre-K:** Establish **Federal infrastructure for universal pre-K**.
  • **Curriculum Mandate:** Mandatory financial literacy and constitutional education in all high schools.

The Stars and Stripes Forever Act: Space and Tech Leadership

The next frontier is space. Investing in NASA creates high-paying jobs and ensures our technological leadership for decades to come.

  • **NASA Budget:** **Double NASA's budget to $30 Billion** annually.
  • **New Space Goals:** Establish a **Moon base by 2022** and a **Mars colony by 2030** (adjusted for 2016 timeframe).
  • **Job Creation:** Generate **200,000 new aerospace jobs** across the Commonwealth.
  • **Infrastructure:** Expand the "Space Coast" with new launch facilities and university space partnerships.
  • **Private Incentives:** Provide **private space industry tax incentives**.

The Governor's Journey

Gavin 's political career has always been driven by an understanding that government must adapt to meet the challenges of the future.

He championed bold reforms as a Mayor, modernized the economic strategy as Lt. Governor, and now brings a renewed focus on massive state investment in infrastructure, education, and energy independence.

A Timeline of Public Service

  • 2000-2004
    Board of Supervisors: Key advocate for progressive housing reforms.
  • 2004-2011
    Mayor: First to advance major social equity and environmental standards.
  • 2011-2019
    Lt. Governor: Focused on state-wide economic development and business climate reform.
  • 2019-Present
    Governor: Championing the largest infrastructure and education investment package in the state's history.
